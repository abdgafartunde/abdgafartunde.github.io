    <div class="editorial-label"><p>Scope</p></div>
    <div class="editorial-content">
      <h2 id="scope-heading">Technical guidance grounded in current research</h2>
      <div class="services-introduction">
        <p>I take on a limited amount of work alongside my academic research. The common thread is applied mathematics: understanding a difficult model, choosing a sound computational method, or developing the knowledge needed to conduct independent research.</p>
        <p>My strongest areas are inverse problems, numerical methods for differential equations, scientific machine learning, regularization, and computational imaging. The <a href="/research/">Research page</a> gives a detailed account of my publications, projects, and current programme.</p>
      </div>

      <div class="services-principles" aria-label="Working principles">
        <article><span>01</span><div><h3>Specific scope</h3><p>We define the question, intended outcome, and limits of the engagement before work begins.</p></div></article>
        <article><span>02</span><div><h3>Technical honesty</h3><p>I work only where my mathematical and computational background is directly useful.</p></div></article>
        <article><span>03</span><div><h3>Independent thinking</h3><p>Guidance is designed to strengthen your own analysis, implementation, and decision-making.</p></div></article>
      </div>
